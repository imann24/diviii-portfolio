GlowLime Games

Summary

GlowLime Games was the main focus of my Division III. Its a for-education game development organization based in the Five Colleges. I founded it during my third year at Hampshire, and worked on it throughout my fourth. My primary goal was to provide students the opportunity to work collaboratively on game development projects. In this regard, I believe the project was a success.
