Centron

Summary

Centron was created during Global Game Jam 2017. It's a small, contained game about shooting incoming space ships to defend earth. It was based off the theme of "waves." Centron was developed as a collaboration between students from Hampshire College and members of the Pioneer Valley Game Developers. I included it in my portfolio as it nicely demonstrates a game which was well scoped to the time frame (24 hours). I also believe the execution was highly successful. This game accomplishes all of the desired design goals and is a distinctly comprehensible project.
