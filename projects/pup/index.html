Pickup Pup

Summary

Pickup Pup is a mobile game focusing on dog collecting. It was created by a Smith College student, Sam Luangkhot, who pitched it through GlowLime Match (a matchmaking service we devised at GlowLime to provide developers with more visibility on their projects). The game began development in December 2016, and we've since shown it Woo! Games Pile and won third place for College Alpha at the MassDiGI Games Challenge. The game is currently midway through Alpha development and we plan to be feature complete by the end of May 2017.
