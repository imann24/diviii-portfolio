Object Factory

Summary

Object Factory is a series of assignments I designed for Jaime Davila's CS112: Intro to Programming in Unity course. I served as a Teaching Assistant in the class. I created these projects in the environment of Unity and structured them around fundamental programming concepts (conditional statements, loops, classes, etc). My hope was to show the capabilities of Unity while also introducing straightforward and sandboxed exercises for students.
