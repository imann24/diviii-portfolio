Winter Studio

Summary

GlowLime Winter Studio was a workshop I organized. We had roughly 14 students participating. The concept was to provides students the opportunity to work full-time on a game project for nearly three weeks. It's a distinct challenge to finish a game in three weeks, and we didn't meet the goal of completing the project. However, we did manage to build a playable game. Many students expressed a preference for having the opportunity to work for full time in a dedicated space with their teammates. Issues with polishing the game itself aside, I believe Winter Studio succeeded as an experiment for demonstrating a different model for game development at Hampshire.
