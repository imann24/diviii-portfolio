Lucid

Summary

Lucid was created by Amherst College student Mashi Zaman. It simulates a phone user interface. The player experiences the game world through the main character's cell phone. The primary interaction is texting to interact with the other characters in the game. Through this medium, the player experiences the narrative of the game.
