Letters to Reid

Summary

Letters to Reid was a narrative game exploring how we deal with grief and loss. It was created by Martha Hollister (a fellow Division III at Hampshire College). The game was developed by Martha solo during Fall 2016 and with a full GlowLime development team in Spring 2017.
