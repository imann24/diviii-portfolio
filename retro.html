Retrospective

The concept for my Division III began as a game idea. However, the more I thought about it, I realized I lacked many of the skills necessary to make a complete game. This morphed into the concept of assembling a team to make such a game, but as I considered this further, I realized Hampshire currently lacked the structure for forming such teams. This spawned the idea that would crystalize into my Division III. GlowLime Games

GlowLime was intended to serve students interested in game development coming from all disciplines and at all levels of experience. It would provide educational, hands-on experience in game development.
